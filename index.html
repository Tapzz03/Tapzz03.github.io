Portfolio

Wild Uchi

Wild Uchi is an E-commerce website dedicated to promoting natural health and wellness. Founded in 2021, the website offers a curated selection of skincare, beauty, and natural food products. The platform leverages technology to provide a user-friendly experience while effectively managing product information and customer interactions.


Technologies used:

The website is built using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for its structure, styling, and interactive elements. PHP is employed for server-side logic and database interactions, with SQLite serving as the database for storing product information and user data. To facilitate development and management, the team utilizes Visual Studio Code as the primary IDE. Afrihost provides the hosting and domain services for the website's online presence. Google Analytics and Google Console used to help boast the online presence.

CampusRes

Campusres is a custom-designed, NSFAS-accredited student residence offering a premium living experience in Ruimsig, Johannesburg. Conveniently located near major universities, shopping centers, and outdoor amenities, Campusres caters to students, athletes, and young professionals seeking a vibrant and secure home away from home.



Technologies used:

The Campusres website was developed collaboratively using WordPress as the Content Management System (CMS) and Elementor as the page builder. WordPress provided the foundational structure and functionality for the website, while Elementor empowered us to create a visually appealing and user-friendly interface with drag-and-drop simplicity. This combination allowed for efficient website management and customization. Afrihost was chosen as the hosting provider to ensure reliable website uptime and performance. Google Analytics was integrated to track website traffic and user behavior, providing valuable insights for optimization and improvement.
